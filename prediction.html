What future may look like?

    The progressive states, including the state of california, have policies to reduce green house gas (GHG) emissions. Transportation sector plays a major role in GHG emission1. One of the policies for pollution mitigation in transportation sector is to expand the number of zero emission vehicles. One of the key factors in policy making, as well as industry, is evaluating the progress toward meeting the set goals. A reasonable milestone for future could not be set but with the help of a good anlysis and modeling of the past and the present state.

    Herein, I have modeled the growth of the numbers of electric vehicles at the streets and the roads of the state of California. A wise modeling approach is to begin with the simplest model and step by step adding to the complexity of the model untill it reaches the satisfactory state. The approach I have taken is universal and would save resources and capital for industries, coporations and governments. Figures below show these steps and the improvement of the model.
