What I do

Here I explain the 4 steps in this project:

I. Collection of raw data

  • Public data from state government in form of over 60 csv files where aggregated, including data of different years, different counties and vehicles stats and emission numbers.
    • > Geographical data was scraped from web

    II. Statistical analysis

    Data were aggregated and statistical analysis where performed on the CO2 emission per year and per type of vehicle

    III. Inteactive map
